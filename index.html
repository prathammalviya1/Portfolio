Soybean Leaf Disease Prediction

Soyabean Leaf Disease Prediction Project is a mobile app that runs on both Android and iOS devices. It is built using Flutter and Dart for the frontend, with the backend powered by CNN and Vanilla GAN. The model performs 7-class classification for soybean leaves using a dataset of 770 images (110 images per class). The app allows users to upload live images from the gallery to predict whether a soybean leaf is diseased or healthy. It also provides the confidence score of the prediction and recommends suitable treatments for the identified disease.

üåü Disease Classification

The model is trained to identify the following soybean leaf conditions:

  • Disease 1: Bacterial Pustule ‚Äì Causes small, raised spots on the leaf surface that may merge, leading to yellowing.
  • Disease 2: Frogeye Leaf Spot ‚Äì Circular lesions with dark borders and gray centers, reducing photosynthesis capacity.
  • Disease 3: Rust ‚Äì Orange to brown pustules on the underside of leaves, leading to early defoliation and yield loss.
  • Disease 4: Sudden Death Syndrome ‚Äì Yellowing between veins followed by leaf drop, often linked to root infection.
  • Disease 5: Target Leaf Spot ‚Äì Brown necrotic spots with concentric rings, resembling a "target-like" pattern.
  • Disease 6: Yellow Mosaic ‚Äì Viral disease causing mosaic-like yellow and green patches, stunting plant growth.
  • Disease 7: Healthy ‚Äì Properly classified as non-diseased leaves with no visible symptoms.
